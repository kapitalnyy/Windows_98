Minesweeper

How to play?

Objective:

- Your goal is to uncover all the squares on the board that do not contain mines.

- Use flags to mark potential mines and avoid clicking on them.

Rules:

- The board consists of hidden squares, some of which contain mines.

- Left-click on a square to reveal it:

- If the square contains a mine, the game is over.

- If the square does not contain a mine, it will show a number or remain blank:

- A number indicates how many mines are in the surrounding 8 squares.

- A blank square (0 nearby mines) will automatically reveal its neighboring squares.

- Right-click on a square to place a flag[F]. Flags help you mark squares you believe contain mines.

How to Play:

- Starting the Game:

- Click any square to begin. The first square is guaranteed to be safe.

- Analyzing Clues:

- Numbers on revealed squares provide hints about the locations of nearby mines.

- Placing Flags:

- If you're confident a square contains a mine, right-click it to place a flag. Flags can be toggled on and off.

- Continuing:

- Keep uncovering squares and marking mines with flags until all safe squares are revealed.

Winning the Game:

- You win when all non-mine squares are revealed.

- Flags are helpful but not mandatory for winning.

Losing the Game:

- The game ends immediately if you left-click on a square with a mine.

Tips and Strategies:

- Use numbers to deduce mine locations:

- For example, if a revealed square shows "1" and is adjacent to only one hidden square, that square must contain a mine.

- Avoid guessing whenever possible. Use logic to determine safe squares and mine locations.

- Be cautious: avoid rushing to click if you're uncertain about a square.

- Early in the game, guessing might be unavoidable, but as the board clears, logic takes over.

Controls:

- Left-click: Reveal a square.

- Right-click: Place or remove a flag.

- Use these controls to alternate between marking potential mines and uncovering safe squares.

Resetting the Game:

- The game will automatically reset if you lose, or you can refresh the page to start a new game.
